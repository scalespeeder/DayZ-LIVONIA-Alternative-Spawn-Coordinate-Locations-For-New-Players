DayZ Livonia Alternative New Player Fresh Spawn Locations & Coordinates XML Mod For Consoles & PC XML Snippets Instructions & Terms Of Use

These snippets will spawn in new players at various alternative LIVONIA locations, depending on which code snippets or complete files you use: Along the South Border, In The Towns, Villages & Cities,
or randomly across the map.

PLEASE NOTE FRESH SPAWNS MAY APPEAR IN GAS LOCATIONS OR AT OTHER UNSUITABLE LOCATIONS, I HAVEN'T TESTED THEM ALL!

Limited Testing on PC Livonia Local Server DAYZ Version 1.20 APRIL 2023.

Instructions:

To download all of the files from the Github, click on the green "Code" button then "download zip" and extract / unzip that file onto your local PC hard-drive.

I have included 1.20 Update Compatible cfgplayerspawnpoints files, you just need to rename them as such and upload them to your LIVONIA (Enoch) Mission file and restart your server.
HOWEVER if you are using this mod beyond update 1.20 it is preferable to overwrite the vanilla player spawn points inside your cfgplayerspawnpoints.xml file with the approriate xml snipet
you can see below, as there may be other changes to the file:
